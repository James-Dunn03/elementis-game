The World of
Elementis
◆

Since the first dawn, the world of Elementis has been held in balance by four great forces — Pyro, the flame that forges, Aqua, the tide that shapes, Terra, the stone that endures, and Aero, the wind that guides. Each force flows through the land, through its creatures, and through all living things. The monsters of this realm are born of these elements: some are fierce and untamed, born to test the strong; others are loyal and steadfast, waiting to be found by a worthy companion. Champions have long walked these lands, forming bonds with elemental creatures and earning their place in its history.

But now a shadow stirs at the edges of the realm. Four Elemental Titans — ancient, colossal, and consumed by fury — have broken free from their long slumber, each one threatening to tear the world apart with the unchecked power of their element. The mountains crack. The seas rise. The winds howl without mercy. The fires burn without end. Unless these titans are brought down, the balance of Elementis will collapse entirely, leaving nothing but ruin. Now it falls to you — alone or alongside fellow champions — to venture forth, forge bonds with the creatures of this realm, and face the titans before the world is lost. The fate of Elementis rests in your hands.

How to Play
A Guide to the Realm
Objective
Venture across the realm and challenge the four territory bosses — one guardian rules each region. Defeat all four bosses before your rivals and claim victory.
Turn Order
Players take turns in sequence. On your turn, click any adjacent hex on the board to move your character there. When you land on a hex you trigger whatever is on that tile — a wild encounter, a village, a camp, or a fate card. End your turn and the next player moves.
Combat
When you land on a hex occupied by a wild monster, battle begins. First, both sides roll dice to determine initiative — the higher roll acts first. Each round, select one of your monster's attacks. Every attack shows the die rolls required to land a hit: roll within those numbers to deal damage.
Elements matter. Each monster has an element — Pyro, Aqua, Terra, or Aero. Attacking with an advantaged element deals double damage; a disadvantaged element deals half.
If your monster falls, you may send in another companion or attempt to flee. Fleeing requires a successful dice roll — roll 4 or higher on a d6 to escape. Win the battle to earn gold and potentially tame the wild monster.
Elemental Strengths
Aqua beats Pyro
Pyro beats Terra
Terra beats Aero
Aero beats Pyro
The Village
Landing on a village hex opens a hub with four services:
Local Goods Supplier — Browse and purchase items and relics to equip on your monsters or use during your journey.
The Inn — Rest and fully restore your HP and all your companions for 50 gold.
Monster Tamer — Trade gold to tame wild monsters and add them to your party, or trade companions you no longer need.
Hire a Ship — Pay 300 gold to hire a ship and crew, granting access to the Misty Islands region and its boss.
Making Camp
When you move to a new hex you have the chance to build a camp there, you need to sacrifice 3 items from your inventory to establish a camp on that hex. Return to your camp at any time to fully restore your HP and all your companions at no cost.
The Traveling Djinn
Every few turns a mysterious Djinn appears on the board looking for a particular item. Land on the Djinn's hex to see what the Djinn is looking for. If you have that item in your inventory, and provide it to the Djinn, the Djinn will offer you something useful for your journey. Once encountered, the Djinn moves on. Only three Djinn will appear on the board at any given time.
How to Win
Each region has one powerful boss guarding it. To challenge a boss, move onto the boss hex in their territory. Defeat all four bosses — the guardians of the Volcan Mountains, Misty Islands, Valley Meadows, and Mountain Forests — and you win the game.
Build your party, stock up on relics, and choose your battles wisely. The realm is unforgiving — come prepared.
